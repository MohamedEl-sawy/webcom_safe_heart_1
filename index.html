About Safe Heart

Smart, real-time cardiac monitoring for proactive healthcare

Safe Heart is an integrated smart healthcare system designed for continuous, real-time heart health monitoring. The solution combines medical-grade wearable sensors (hardware) with a mobile application (software) to collect, analyze, and visualize vital cardiac data in a clear and user-friendly manner.

The system continuously measures heart rate and related vital indicators, transmitting data instantly to the mobile app, where readings are analyzed and categorized into three health states: Normal, Warning, and Critical. In case of abnormal readings, Safe Heart automatically triggers real-time alerts to ensure immediate attention.

During emergency situations, the system can automatically notify caregivers or guardians and securely share patient data with a dedicated hospital web dashboard. This allows medical teams to review the patient’s condition and medical history in advance, accelerating clinical decision-making and enabling timely first aid and intervention.

Safe Heart targets heart patients, elderly individuals, children, and healthcare providers. The system is designed to be affordable, easy to use, and requires minimal user interaction—especially important for vulnerable groups such as seniors and children.

Built with scalability in mind, Safe Heart can be expanded in the future by integrating artificial intelligence technologies to analyze health patterns and predict potential risks before they occur. This contributes to improved healthcare quality, reduced sudden emergencies, and supports the digital transformation of the medical sector.
